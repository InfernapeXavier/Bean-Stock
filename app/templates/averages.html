Averages

About Averages

SMA The simple moving average (SMA) calculates an average of the last n prices, where n represents the number of periods for which you want the average.
WMA The weighted moving average (WMA) gives you a weighted average of the last n prices, where the weighting decreases with each previous price. This works similarly to the EMA, but you calculate the WMA differently.
EMA The exponential moving average (EMA) is a weighted average of the last n prices, where the weighting decreases exponentially with each previous price/period. In other words, the formula gives recent prices more weight than past prices.
